Alert

Alerts are used to gain the user attraction for some important piece of information without interrupting user flow.

Amaze-UI has different kinds of alerts.

Standard Alerts

Standard alert has four variants alert-success , alert-warning , alert-danger , alert-info.You need to use class as "alert" & the corresponding variant of it as shown in example

Outline Alerts

Outline alerts also has four different varaiants outline-success , outline-warning , outline-danger , outline-info.Use the corresponding outline class with the alert class.
